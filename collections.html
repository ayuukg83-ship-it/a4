Questions

Before you add anything

Do I need every item in the edit?

No. Use the edit to identify gaps and adapt ideas to your routine, budget and taste.

How do I know if a piece is good quality?

Check fabric composition, seam finish, lining, hardware, care needs and how the garment moves.

Does timeless mean boring or neutral?

Never. Timeless style can include colour, print and bold accessories when they feel authentically yours.
