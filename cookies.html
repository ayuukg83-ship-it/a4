Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains how Timeless Style Hub uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored by a browser. Similar technologies such as local storage may remember choices or measure website use.

2. Necessary technologies

A necessary local-storage preference may remember whether you accepted all cookies or selected necessary-only, so the banner is not shown on every page.

3. Analytics

If enabled, analytics cookies help us understand popular pages, errors, devices and referral sources. They should activate only after consent where required.

4. Consent choices

Choose “Accept all” to allow optional analytics or “Necessary only” to keep them disabled. You can reset your choice by clearing this site’s browser data.

5. Third parties

Embedded media, analytics or linked services may set their own technologies subject to their privacy policies.

6. Contact

Cookie questions may be sent to privacy@timelessstylehub.com.
