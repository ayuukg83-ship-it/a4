Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains how Timeless Style Hub collects, uses and protects information when you browse, subscribe or contact us.

1. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide, including your name, email address and message. With consent, we may also collect page visits, approximate location, device type, referral source and cookie identifiers.

2. How we use information

We use information to respond to enquiries, provide requested updates, operate and secure the website, understand useful content and meet legal obligations. Marketing is sent only where permitted, and you can unsubscribe at any time.

3. Cookies and analytics

Optional analytics technologies are disabled until you consent. Necessary local storage may remember your cookie preference. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited information may be shared with hosting, email, analytics or professional service providers under appropriate obligations, or where required by law.

5. Retention and security

Information is retained only as long as needed for its purpose and legal requirements. Reasonable safeguards are used, although no online system is completely secure.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection, and may withdraw consent. Contact privacy@timelessstylehub.com.

7. Children

The website is directed to a general adult audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable minimum age.

8. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@timelessstylehub.com. We may verify identity before completing a request.
